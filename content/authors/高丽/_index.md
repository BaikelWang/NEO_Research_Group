### Research Highlight 
1.	Based on the AI for Science paradigm, developed bidirectional tandem neural networks, Fourier convolution combined with multi-bit digital coding algorithms to efficiently inverse-design metasurfaces capable of multi-dimensional optical field regulation, driving transformative advancements in optical design methodologies.
2.	Developed novel low-dimensional semiconductor heterojunction materials for silicon integration, enhancing light absorption, carrier separation, and optoelectronic performance via interface/band engineering, achieving optimized responsivity, detectivity, speed, and multi-functional applications in high-speed imaging and optoelectronic memory device.  
3.	Developed wafer-scale metasurface fabrication techniques for high-sensitivity spectral sensing and photodetection enhancement. By integrating silicon metasurfaces with photodetectors and employing compressive sensing/neural networks, a miniaturized computational spectrometer with 0.4 nm spectral resolution and AI-driven spectral-to-material characterization are demonstrated.
